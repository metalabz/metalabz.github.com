OUR SERVICES


Consulting

We begin with simple questions: What is your product? What do you want to achieve? How strategic is IT in your business ? We explore your needs and find the right technologies for your project.

Design

This is where the engineering begins. An application quality hinges upon two things: a well designed software and a reliable infrastructure. We create a strategic plan that outlines what we will make together, how make the most of your budget and what success looks like.

Development

We work hard to offer you the best quality for your application. We start loose, develop parts of your product, iterate, and refine them through regular dialogue with you. At the end you get a thoroughly tested and maintenable software created with the best technologies of the moment.

Launch

For us, this means a reliable automated and scalable infrastructure. We choose the right hosting solutions for you and ensure that your webapp is always up and running.

ABOUT US


Meet the founders

The company was founded in September 2012. The founders are brilliant engineers.

Yann

Yann is a talented system & network engineer and a big cloud computing fan. His heart beats alternatively for system and software engineering. He loves snowboarding, but Paris lacks a little bit of snow and mountain.

Yann studied IT in France and in California, USA. While in California, he had the opportunity to study entrepreneurship. Having worked with cloud startups in France and in the US, he realized that system and development where too often disconnected. MetaLabz was born.

Bastien

Bastien is a sharp software engineer and web fanatic. He loves being challenged and is always pushing the boundaries of new technologies. He enjoys getting away from the office to shoot some pictures in Paris and to discover historical places.

Bastien studied IT in France and in the UK. Always looking for challenge, he realized that the startup world was made for him. After some work with french startups, he decided that it was his turn to help France innovate. MetaLabz was born.
